I purchased all the parts individually because it gave me some flexibility. If you are new to speaker building I would recommend purchasing a speaker kit. These speakers are passive speakers, meaning they need an integrated audio amplifer for power. The amplifier, woodworking, and soldering tools are not included in the pricing.

Overview

This website documents John Fattore's summer 2022 stereo speaker build

The build consist of 2 floorstanding speakers, made of cherry, maple, and MDF

The crossover seperates out the signal, sending the highs to the tweeter and the lows to the woofers

Assembly takes 40+ parts and makes it into 2 speakers

Observe different parts of the build though the top navigation bar
